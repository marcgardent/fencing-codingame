The Goal

Hit your opponent and manage our energy to score 20 points!

  Rules

Actions


Two players choose simultaneously only one action among fives: break and walk and retreat and lunge and parry. in details:

name code energy move long
BREAK 1 2 0 0
WALK 2 -1 30 0
RETREAT 3 -1 -30 0
LUNGE (attack) 4 -2 0 30
PARRY (defense) 5 -2 0 -30
Added in the second league:
name code energy move long
MIDDLE_POSTURE 6 -1 0 0
TOP_POSTURE 7 -1 0 0
BOTTOM_POSTURE 8 -1 0 0
  • code: number used to communicate with the referee.
  • energy: consume and produce in the range [ 0 , 20 ].
  • move: apply a move of your character in the range [ 0 , 500 ].
  • long: positive is an attack; negative is a defense; see Â§Assault.

Resolutions

  • The players respawn when they collide (myPosition > yourPosition) or an assault is succeeded.
  • The players score both when they touch simultaneously ( LUNGE vs LUNGE are resolved).
  • Your action is suppressed and the rest of your energy is spent when you request an action without enough energy.
  • Your move is resolved as far as possible and energy spent when you exceed the boundaries.

Assaults


Assault resolution depends of longs and positions:

isTouchedWhenAttackDefense(strikerPosition:int, defenderPosition:int) -> (defender + Parry_Range) + (striker + Lunge_Range) > 500;

isTouchedWhenAttack(strikerPosition:int, defenderPosition:int) -> (defender) + (striker + Lunge_Range) > 500;

to use it, Normalize the position of your opponent, two sample bellow:

yourPositionNormalized = (500 - yourPosition)

myAttackYouDefend = isTouchedWhenAttackDefense(myPosition, yourPositionNormalized)

youAttack = isTouchedWhenAttack(yourPositionNormalized, myPosition)

  Game I/O

Input for one game turn
Line 1:myPosition myEnergy myScore myPosture
Line 2:yourPosition yourEnergy yourScore yourPosture
  • myPosition, yourPosition: range [ 0 , 500 ], respawn (me: 200 , you: 300 )
  • myEnergy, yourEnergy: range [ 0 , 20 ], init 20
  • myScore, yourScore: range [ 0 , 20 ], init 0
  • myPosture, yourPosture: MIDDLE_POSTURE = 6 , TOP_POSTURE = 7 , BOTTOM_POSTURE = 8
Output for one game turn
Line 1:myAction
possibles values:
  • BREAK = 1
  • WALK = 2
  • RETRAIT = 3
  • LUNGE = 4
  • PARRY = 5
  • MIDDLE_POSTURE = 6
  • TOP_POSTURE = 7
  • BOTTOM_POSTURE = 8
Victory Conditions
  • Score more points than your opponent(s) at games end.
  • The final result for your ranking equals myScore - YourScore.
Loss Conditions
  • Score fewer points than your opponent(s) at games end.
  • You do not respond in due time or output an invalid command.
